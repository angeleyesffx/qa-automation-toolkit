About

Building reliable software through quality engineering

Priscilla R. Martins is a QA Engineer and Test Automation Specialist with a long track record of supporting product quality in complex delivery environments. Her experience covers test planning, exploratory testing, automation design, BDD workflows, regression coverage, and performance testing for teams working with customer-facing and business-critical systems.

Her technical toolkit includes Python, Ruby, JavaScript, Scala, C, Cucumber, Selenium, WebdriverIO, Pytest, Behave, Gatling, and mobile testing practices. This breadth supports a practical quality strategy that balances fast feedback, maintainability, and confidence in release cycles.
